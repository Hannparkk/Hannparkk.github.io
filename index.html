About Me



I'm a high school civics teacher. Over the years, I have send hundreds of kids to local board meetings. I've decided it's time to take my own advice and get actively involved in the local decision making process.

I have lived in Wheaton for 22 years. Wheaton is a great community to live in. I teach my students, and I personally believe, that local government has the greatest impact on our daily lives. The current board has made some very solid decisions to move Wheaton forward as a vibrant and modern community. I want to preserve and build on what we have accomplished as citizents
